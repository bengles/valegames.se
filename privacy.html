Privacy Policy

Your privacy is really important to us at Vale Games. Therefore we are glad to give you this privacy policy to let you know what data we collect and what we do with it.

Account Information

When you create an account for World of Pixels the only information that Vale Games accesses is your email bound to the login service of your choice, be it Facebook or Google. No other information is accessed or stored. The email is stored for communications purposes.

Gameplay Information

When you have created an account for World of Pixels all gameplay statistics needed to provide the gameplay experiences is stored, such as items, coins and pixels.
