World of Pixels

World of Pixels turns the world into a large canvas which artist is all the people on the globe. Let your creative mind color all of your favorite places together with your friends. However, be prepared to defend your artwork from other hungry artists in the world.

KTH Campus

KTH Campus is a game that gives the players an interactive tour of the KTH Royal Institute of Technology campus. Available on App Store and Google Play.
